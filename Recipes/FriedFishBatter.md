## Introduction

This is a pretty tasty batter for frying good fish filets in raps oil.

## Ingredients

*   2 tsp. salt
*   1/2 tsp. black peppar
*   1 cup flour
*   1 tblsp. garlic powder
*   1 tblsp. paprika
*   1 egg
*   12 oz / 33 cl. beer
*   1 liter or quart raps oil for frying.
